# DarkReader Support

Welcome to DarkReader support! This page will help you get the most out of your e-reader experience.

## Getting Started

### Importing Books
1. Tap the **+** button in the bottom right of your Library
2. Browse and select your file (PDF, EPUB, TXT, CBZ, ZIP, or images)
3. Optionally edit the book title
4. Tap **Add** to import to your library

### Reading Books
- **Tap right side** of the screen to turn to the next page
- **Tap left side** to go back to the previous page
- **Tap center** to show/hide controls and page counter

### Adjusting Font Size
1. While reading, tap the **center** of the screen
2. Use **A-** to decrease font size
3. Use **A+** to increase font size
4. *(Note: Font size only works for text-based files like EPUB and TXT)*

## Features

### Progress Tracking
Your reading progress is automatically saved:
- Progress bars appear in your Library
- Resume reading where you left off
- Works for all file types

### Chapter Detection (EPUB/TXT)
DarkReader automatically detects and formats chapter titles:
- Looks for "Chapter 1", "Part 2", etc.
- Displays chapters in larger, bold text
- Centers chapter titles for emphasis

### Dark Theme
The entire app uses a dark theme designed for comfortable reading:
- Black background
- White text
- Gray accents
- Easy on your eyes

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why isn't my EPUB loading?
Make sure your EPUB file is not corrupted. Some EPUBs are image-based rather than text-based, which may cause loading issues.

### Can I change the background color?
Currently, DarkReader only supports the dark theme (black background with white text).

### Where are my books stored?
All books and reading progress are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud or shared externally.

### Can I organize books into folders?
Not yet! This feature is planned for a future update.

### Does DarkReader collect my data?
No! DarkReader does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is our priority.
